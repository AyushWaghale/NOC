Our Mission

  • To protect life and property in Delhi NCR through prevention and emergency response
  • To ensure fire safety compliance in the national capital

Our History

The Maharashtra Fire Service has been serving the state since its establishment under the Maharashtra Fire Prevention and Life Safety Measures Act, 2006. It operates as a crucial emergency response organization dedicated to protecting citizens and property from fire-related incidents.

The service has evolved from its humble beginnings to become one of India's most advanced fire fighting forces, incorporating modern technology and best practices in fire safety and prevention.

Our Leadership

The Maharashtra Fire Service is led by the Director of Maharashtra Fire Services, supported by a team of experienced fire officers and administrative staff. Our leadership team brings decades of combined experience in fire fighting, disaster management, and public safety.
